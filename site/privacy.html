Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Roastlog - Home Roasting Journal is an offline-first app. All data you enter (entries, notes, ratings) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area and is never transmitted to any server, and no analytics or tracking services are used.

The app does not collect, sell, or share any personal information. In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it.
